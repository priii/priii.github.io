Application Process

  1. Home
  2. Admission

We welcome your interest in our parent cooperative preschool, where parents and teachers work together to make our program a success.

Our school year runs from September to May. We also offer summer camps in-between. While our normal application process for the upcoming school year starts on the day of our open house in January of each year, we do accept applications year-roundand allow children to join classes mid-year if spots are available.

We are accepting applications for the current term 2017/18. Please feel free to contact us to inquire about a specific class.

1. Download the MVPNS Application Form Application Form

2. Please submit your application with the non-refundable application fee of $65.

3. If you are offered a spot at MVPNS, please submit a non-refundable family placement fee to secure a spot in the program within two weeks of your acceptance. Placement fees are $200 for parent and child classes and $350 for coop classes payable to MVPNS. If enrolling in multiple classes the family placement fee will be at the higher rate.


In accordance with the policy of the Mountain View-Los Altos Union High School District, MVPNS does not discriminate in its educational programs, activities, or employment practices with respect to ethnic group, religion, gender, color, race, national origin or physical or mental disability.

If you have any questions, please contact MVPNS Membership at (408) 883-KIDS or info@mvpns.org

Thank you for your interest in Mountain View Parent Nursery School!
