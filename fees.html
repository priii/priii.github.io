Fees and Tuition for the 2017-2018 school year will be as follows:

1. Non-refundable application fee of $65 is due with completed application.

2. Non-refundable family placement fee of $200 for parent and child classes and $350 for coop classes due immediately upon acceptance to secure a place in the program for 2017-2018. If enrolling in multiple classes the family placement fee will be at the higher rate.

3. A non-refundable first tuition payment is also due immediately upon acceptance into the program for 2017-2018. This will apply to the tuition for September 2017. Tuition is paid one month ahead, at the beginning of each month through April 2018.
