Summer Camp at the Moon Site 2017

Week of June 6- Week of June 27

3 days per week(Tuesday,Wednesday,Thursday) 8:45am-11:45am

4 week-by-week sessions $150 per session

Older 3's-Younger 5's*

Are you ready for your child to GET MESSY this summer with water, sand and paint? Register for summer camp at MVPNS!

Children who are currently enrolled in the 3's, 4's, and 4/5's during the 2015-2016 school year have priority registration through Friday, March 17th. We will then open up our summer camp registration to the public.

Please mail the completed registration form to: MVPNS, P.O. Box 4174, Mountain View, CA 94040. Attach a check made out to MVPNS in the amount of $150 per week. Please note the name of your child and "Summer Camp" in the memo section of your check.

Tuition is non-refundable unless we can replace your child's spot. Limited tuition assistance is available.

Please note: We are still developing the number of days required for the work parent schedule during camp (maximum will be one day per week).

If you have any questions please contact Teacher Claire via email at claire@mvpns.org.

* Must have turned 3 years old by September 1, 2016
