MVPNS is a self-funded non-profit parent participation nursery school. One of our goals is to keep our school financially accessible to families with lower income by providing tuition assistance. Support from the community helps to make this possible. In addition to having fundraising events every year, we encourage people to join the eScrip program, and name Mountain View Parent Nursery School as the beneficiary. You can sign up by clicking the icon below.

If you would like to make a direct donation, please contact our treasurer at operations@mvpns.org. All donations are tax deductible. You may designate that your donation be used in an area of interest to you. We appreciate your help!
